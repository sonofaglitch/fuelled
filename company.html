Company

Scarlett Turner

Creative Director

Scarlett is a graduate of Brighton (2013) and De Montfort University (2015). Their practice focuses on physical theatre and contemporary dance methods. Currently Scarlett is a breakthrough artists at Curve Theatre and is Artistic Director of Fuelled Dance Theatre that is supported by Attenborough Arts Center. In addition to working with Fuelled Dance Theatre they are currently working on a physical theatre solo identify societies norms, gender and sexuality, called Come as you are. In September this year they workshopped this work to international artists as part of a dance residency in Austria.
